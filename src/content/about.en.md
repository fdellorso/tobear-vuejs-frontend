# Why toBear

toBear was born from a simple idea: todo lists should be fast, direct, and frictionless.

It's inspired by [Clear](https://www.realmacsoftware.com/clear/), the iOS app that for years defined what it means to manage tasks with gestures — one swipe to complete, one to delete, a long press to reorder. Clear has stayed frozen in time: iOS only, never updated. toBear wants to be its multiplatform successor: the same gestural philosophy on mobile, its own identity on desktop.

**Local by choice, not by limitation.** toBear works completely offline, without requiring an account. Your tasks live on your device. If you ever want to sync them across multiple devices, you can create an account at any time — the tasks you've already created will come with you.

**Open source.** toBear's code is public on [GitHub](https://github.com/fdellorso/tobear-vuejs-frontend) — you can see how it's built, report an issue, or contribute.

toBear is a personal project, built and maintained by one person. If you like it, let me know.
